# Moneyhub — Product (target)

**Register:** brand

## Users
B2B decision-makers at financial institutions — product, CX, data, and innovation leaders at banks, lenders, building societies, pension providers, and wealth/advice firms. Secondary: developers integrating the APIs; partners and press.

## Product Purpose
Moneyhub is an open-finance / open-banking **data platform**: it collects, enriches, and contextualises customer financial data so institutions can deliver intelligent, personalised journeys and better customer outcomes. The site sells two solution families (Banking & Lending; Pension & Wealth), a set of underlying data products, and proves them with named customer case studies.

## Brand Personality
Confident, credible, plain-spoken, outcome-oriented; enterprise-grade but human ("Build for real life"). One energetic orange accent on a restrained slate-and-white base; clean square geometry signals precision and trust; real lifestyle photography keeps the data story grounded.

## Design Principles
1. **One accent, used sparingly** — orange earns attention because everything else is slate and white.
2. **Square and clean** — 0px corners on structure; contrast from full-bleed color blocks, not shadows.
3. **Outcome-led copy** — short imperative headlines, benefit-first body. (Content is migrated verbatim from the source; never out-author it.)
4. **Proof everywhere** — customer logos, named case studies, quotes.
5. **Fast and accessible** — token-driven, WCAG AA, Lighthouse 100.

## Information Architecture
Preserved **verbatim** from the live site (this is a migration): Home · Solutions (Banking & Lending, Pension & Wealth + detail pages) · Products (overview + detail) · Use cases · Discover (Articles, Glossary, Webinars) · Case studies · Press & partners · About · Careers · Policies · Contact / Demo request.

## Anti-references
No rounded/bubbly consumer-app styling, no heavy gradients or glow, no abstract 3D stock, no jargon walls. Institutional-but-warm, not playful-fintech.
