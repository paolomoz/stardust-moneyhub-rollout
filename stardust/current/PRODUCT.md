# Moneyhub — Product (current state)

**Register:** brand

## Users
Primary audience is **B2B decision-makers at financial institutions** — product, CX, data, and innovation leaders at banks, lenders, building societies, pension providers, and wealth/advice firms. Secondary: developers integrating the APIs, and partners/press.

## Product Purpose
Moneyhub is an **open-finance / open-banking data platform**. It "collects, enriches and contextualises customer data so that financial institutions can drive better customer outcomes." The site sells two solution families (Banking & Lending; Pension & Wealth) and a set of underlying data products (Data Aggregation, Categorisation & Enrichment Engine, Data Explorer, Data Insights & Recommendations, Payments, Embedded Solutions & Portals), evidenced by customer case studies.

## Brand Personality
Confident, credible, plain-spoken, outcome-oriented. Enterprise-grade but human ("Build for real life"). Restrained palette with a single energetic orange accent; clean square-edged geometry signals precision and trustworthiness. Lifestyle photography keeps the data story grounded in real customers.

## Information Architecture (observed)
- **Home** — positioning, trusted-by logos, two solution teasers, products grid, case-study proof, closing CTA.
- **Solutions** — two overview pages (Banking & Lending, Pension & Wealth) each with ~6 detail sub-pages (Smart Segmentation, Smart Lending, Targeted Support, Win Schemes, …).
- **Products** — overview + 6 product detail pages.
- **Use cases** — listing + detail pages.
- **Discover / Articles** — blog/article listing + posts; Glossary; Webinars.
- **Press and partners** — press-release listing + detail pages.
- **Case studies** — listing + detail pages (customer stories).
- **About / Careers** — company.
- **Policies** — privacy, ESG, modern slavery, security & trust, terms, API terms, status.
- **Contact / Demo request** — forms.

## Content Types / Templates (archetypes)
landing (home, solution overviews) · program (solution detail, product detail, use-case detail) · listing (products, use-cases, case-studies, articles, press, webinars, glossary) · article (case study, blog post, press release, glossary entry) · form (contact, demo request) · static (about, careers, policy/legal).

## Design Principles (inferred from the live site)
1. **One accent, used sparingly** — orange earns attention because everything else is slate and white.
2. **Square and clean** — 0px corners, near-flat surfaces; contrast comes from full-bleed color blocks, not shadows.
3. **Outcome-led copy** — short imperative headlines, benefit-first body.
4. **Proof everywhere** — customer logos, named case studies, quotes.

## Anti-references
Avoid: rounded/bubbly consumer-app styling, heavy gradients and glow, stock-y abstract 3D, dense jargon walls. The brand reads institutional-but-warm, not playful-fintech.
