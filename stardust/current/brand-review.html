Tensions (3)

Sharp vs. soft
Corners are square (0px) but buttons carry a tiny 2px radius and avatars are circular — the rounding language is inconsistent. Pick one radius scale.
og:image is the logo
The home og:image is the Moneyhub logo PNG, not a hero image. Share cards will look thin; the redesign should set a real social image per page.
Consent banner pollutes the fold
The cookie banner overlays the hero and its buttons dominate the CTA inventory — accounted for during extraction, but a sign the consent UX competes with the value prop.
