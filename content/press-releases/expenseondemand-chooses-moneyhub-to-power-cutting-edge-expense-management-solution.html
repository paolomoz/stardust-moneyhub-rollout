ExpenseOnDemand chooses Moneyhub to power cutting-edge expense management solution

  • Uncategorised

Moneyhub has partnered with leading expense management platform, ExpenseOnDemand, to power a seamless, automated expense management experience. This new collaboration will help transform the way businesses handle credit card expenses by integrating intelligent Open Banking technology with a high level of customer support.

Since 2003, ExpenseOnDemand has been at the forefront of transforming expense management, replacing outdated, manual processes with seamless, automated solutions. Unlike rigid, one-size-fits-all platforms, it empowers businesses with unmatched flexibility, exceptional customer support, and fair, transparent pricing—delivering truly tailored expense management experiences.

Driven by client demand for more reliable and broader Open Banking connections, enhanced functionality, and deeper financial insights, ExpenseOnDemand has made the strategic decision to move over to Moneyhub as their provider of Open Banking. This move strengthens their offering with broader aggregation, superior categorisation and enrichment, and best-in-class support—ensuring their clients benefit from a more powerful, efficient, and future-ready expense solution.

Siddharth Nigam, Director of Product and Strategy at ExpenseOnDemand, comments: “Our integration with Moneyhub is a game-changer, bringing seamless credit card connectivity to ExpenseOnDemand across the UK, EU, and Australia. With real-time syncing and enhanced visibility, businesses can automate expense management like never before—saving time, reducing errors, and gaining full financial control.”

Dan Scholey, Chief Commercial Officer at Moneyhub, adds: “We are thrilled to partner with ExpenseOnDemand, a pioneer in the expense management industry. The Moneyhub Platform is designed to deliver accurate, reliable data, along with exceptional support, and integrating this into ExpenseOnDemand’s platform is a great example of how these elements can transform business operations.”

Since partnering with Moneyhub, ExpenseOnDemand is exploring further innovations to enrich their platform.
