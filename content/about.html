Building for real life

Our Mission

Moneyhub empowers leading banks, pension providers, wealth managers and insurers to deliver intelligent, personalised financial journeys that build for real life, driving better outcomes and long-term customer relationships.

With an API-first platform, Moneyhub offers enriched transaction data, AI-driven categorisation, decisioning, analytics, and payment initiation services. These embedded data services and applications help clients attract and retain customers, reduce operational costs, increase profitability, and improve financial wellbeing. Moneyhub is regulated by the FCA and certified to ISO 27001, underscoring its commitment to security, compliance, and innovation.

Our accreditations & certifications

Powering the future of proactive finance

In a complex financial world, success is driven by proactive, personalised action.

Moneyhub is the intelligent engine enabling service providers to shift toward agentic workflows—where financial guidance is always on and optimised.

Our expertise is built on three core pillars:

1. Comprehensive Data

Securely connecting to the widest array of consented financial data to provide the complete, holistic customer view.

2. Proprietary Intelligence

Applying proprietary AI and machine learning, a practice we’ve pioneered since the 2010s, to identify individual opportunities and automate insights. Our market-first algorithms for categorisation and enrichment are testament to this leadership.

3. Controlled Oversight

We operate with a controlled approach to AI, believing in human judgment for critical decisions. Our models deploy with a human-in-the-loop for careful oversight, ensuring ethical deployment, risk mitigation, and superior, trustworthy outcomes.

We deliver the platform that transforms data into lifelong financial well-being for your customers and drives higher lifetime value for your business as you build for real life.

Our commitment: trust, compliance and ethical innovation

Moneyhub operates within a robust and evolving legislative landscape. We ensure transparent, consent-driven access to data and align with regulations. Our commitment is to deliver better outcomes through a safe, compliant, and accurate solution.

We are FCA-regulated as a Payment Initiation Service Provider (PISP), an Account Information Service Provider (AISP) and a Credit Information Services Provider (CISP). Our systems are certified as ISO27001-compliant to securely manage customer data and protect privacy.
