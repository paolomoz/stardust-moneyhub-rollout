The CTO's Roadmap to AI Personalisation in Financial Services: Governance, Latency and Stack Modernisation

The CTO’s Roadmap to AI Personalisation in Financial Services: Governance, Latency and Stack Modernisation

Available on demand

Exclusive Q&A style webinar with Moneyhub CTO, Dave Tonge

Webinar overview:

Most financial institutions are sitting on massive data lakes, yet the customer experience often remains static. The challenge isn’t acquiring data, it’s enriching it at enterprise scale to deliver insights that actually matter.

Join Dave Tonge, CTO at Moneyhub, for a discussion on the “CTO’s Roadmap” for AI-driven personalisation in financial services. We will explore the engineering realities of implementing predictive context in complex financial environments.

Who is the webinar for:

This session is designed as a structured roadmap for leaders in Technology, Data, Product, and Architecture.

The webinar will focus on three key areas:

  • The Reality Check: Governance & Trust Moving beyond simple categorisation to high-accuracy enrichment. We will discuss the non-negotiable need for data precision to avoid “hallucinations” and ensure compliance when automating financial advice.
  • The Architecture: From Raw Data to Predictive Context How to build the infrastructure that transforms messy, siloed transaction data into actionable intelligence. We will look at integration patterns that handle scale, latency, and diverse data sources efficiently.
  • The ROI: Operationalising Insights How to translate enriched data into tangible business benefits. We will explore specific use cases, from affordability assessments to proactive wellness, that turn “users” into loyal customers.

Our expert speaker:

Dave is a widely recognised authority on data and financial APIs. He has been instrumental in developing the Open Banking standards in the UK that are now used globally.

He serves as Co-Chair of the Financial-grade API (FAPI) Working Group at the OpenID Foundation and is a member of the Open Savings, Investment and Pensions (OSIP) Steering Group at TISA.
