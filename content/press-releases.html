The latest press releases from Moneyhub.

  • Banking

Moneyhub becomes first participant in the UK Payments Initiative

Moneyhub has become the first non-shareholder participant to join UK Payments Initiative Ltd (UKPI). The partnership will allow Moneyhub to bring advanced, rule-based Pay By Bank capabilities to the pensions, investments, and savings sectors. By automating intelligent account-to-account top-ups in real time via UKPI’s Wave 1 framework, Moneyhub will help financial institutions boost assets under management, cut operational costs, and improve customer retention.

Moneyhub and Brick Rewards turn rent into rewards for UK tenants

Moneyhub has partnered with Brick Rewards to help London renters turn rent payments into financial benefits. Using open banking and advanced transaction categorisation, the Brick Rewards app automatically verifies rent payments, enabling users to earn rewards, build their credit history through rent reporting, and improve long-term financial resilience with a seamless, frictionless experience.

Latest Moneyhub release puts an end to unrecognised transaction panic

Moneyhub has unveiled the next generation of its Categorisation and Enrichment Engine, helping financial institutions eliminate unknown transaction panic through enhanced geolocation, accurate merchant identification, richer transaction metadata, and real-time processing that improves customer recognition and supports AI-driven financial services.

Moneyhub Partners with The Tax Guys to Help Landlords and Sole Traders Navigate Making Tax Digital

Moneyhub has partnered with The Tax Guys to help landlords and sole traders prepare for Making Tax Digital ahead of the April 2026 deadline. By powering the Clean Books platform with AI-driven data categorisation and enrichment, the collaboration simplifies compliance, automates financial data collection, and enables more proactive, year-round tax planning.

Nationwide Building Society selects Moneyhub as its data enrichment and categorisation partner

Nationwide Building Society has selected Moneyhub to categorise and enrich all transactions across its 16 million customers. Moneyhub's AI-driven engine will provide granular spending insights, helping customers better manage their finances and quickly identify fraudulent transactions.
